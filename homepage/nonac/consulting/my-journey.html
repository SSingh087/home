It's important to recognize that these moments of disappointment, while discouraging at the time, have ultimately strengthened my applications and, when properly honed, led to success.

Along my academic path, I was fortunate to be the recipient of prestigious scholarships, including IPIASMUS and ERASMUS+, and a secured funding for PhD.
