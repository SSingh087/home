FEES

The service is offered at a modest fee, recognizing that aspiring to pursue graduate studies can be financially demanding. This journey entails various costs, such as application fees, visa expenses, accommodation deposits, and, in worst-case scenarios, tuition fees.

I believe in equitable access to education and am willing to work with you to find a solution that fits your circumstances. I am fully aware of these financial burdens and, in some cases, open to providing our services free of charge, provided there is a genuine need.

It's important to note that funds generated through these services are reinvested into the development of two valuable initiatives: Bose.X and GRA-WITTY. These projects are focused on advancing education and opportunities for aspiring scholars, ultimately positively impacting the academic community.

I invite you to book a call to learn more about the services offered and to discuss your unique needs and circumstances.
