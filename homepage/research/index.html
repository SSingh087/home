GW lensing occurs due to the interaction between GWs and the spacetime curvature generated by massive objects. The curvature of spacetime, caused by the presence of mass and energy, acts as a refractive medium (a lens)that alters the trajectory of GWs.

Much of my work has been centered around exploring the paramter space of the lensed GWs, lens being fuzzy dark matter (FDM).
